Our Phased Action Plan

PHASE 1 (0-3 MONTHS)

Quick Wins

Promote ADUs, streamline conversions of commercial space, and pass initial renter protections like anti-harassment rules.

PHASE 2 (3-9 MONTHS)

Build Momentum

Launch pilot programs for 4-6 story housing on major corridors like Torrance Blvd & PCH. Establish small-site acquisition funds.

PHASE 3 (9-24 MONTHS)

Bigger Moves

Plan high-density housing around the future C-Line station. Launch social housing projects and expand Right to Counsel for tenants.
